The Sales Research Agent

Before every outreach, a good sales rep researches the prospect. Company background, key people, recent news, potential pain points.

That takes 15-20 minutes per prospect. Multiply by 10 prospects per week, and your rep is spending 3+ hours just on research. Before writing a single email.

The Sales Research Agent does it in 60 seconds.

Give it a company name or LinkedIn URL. It generates a complete briefing.
 

What the briefing includes

• Company overview — size, industry, funding, tech stack

• Key decision-makers and their roles

• Recent news and company moves

• Potential pain points based on their situation

• Suggested angle for outreach

• Personalized outreach draft

The DIY version

Use Claude's Research mode to gather public info on a prospect. Then paste the results into a Project that has your sales playbook and ICP loaded. Ask it to draft outreach.

Two steps instead of one, but it works for low volume.
The readiness check

This agent needs: a documented sales playbook, defined ICP (ideal customer profile), brand voice guide, and CRM data.

Here's the thing: without a documented playbook and ICP, AI writes generic outreach. Generic outreach is worse than no outreach — it trains prospects to ignore you.

The playbook comes first. The AI comes second.
The gap between DIY and professional

DIY: Decent research on publicly available data. Manual copy-paste workflow. Fine for 5 prospects a week.

Professional: Checks your CRM for past interactions first, cross-references your ICP for fit scoring, uses your sales playbook for messaging strategy, applies your brand voice for tone, and integrates with your email tool for one-click send. Rep says "Research Acme Corp" and gets a complete, ready-to-send package.
