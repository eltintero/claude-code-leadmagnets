The Pattern

Every good delegation has 4 parts.

  • 01
    Where to look — "Read the brief in my Proposals folder"
  • 02
    What to follow — "Use our standard template"
  • 03
    How it should sound — "Match the tone of the Meridian proposal"
  • 04
    What to produce — "Save a draft in the right folder"

Give Claude these 4 things and the output changes completely.
