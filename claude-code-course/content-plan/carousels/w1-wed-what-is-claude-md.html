The Template

Takes 15 minutes to write.

# About Our Business

## Who We Are
[What you do, who you serve]

## How We Talk
- Tone: confident, warm, never corporate
- Never say: "innovative" or "cutting-edge"

## Important Rules
- NEVER discount below 15%
- Always include timeline in proposals
