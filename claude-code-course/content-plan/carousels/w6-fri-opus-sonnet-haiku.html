How to Think About It

3 employees at different hourly rates. Assign work accordingly.

  • You wouldn't pay your senior consultant to reformat a spreadsheet
  • You wouldn't send a junior to present to the board
  • You wouldn't use Opus for a quick email reply
  • You wouldn't use Haiku for a $50K proposal

Match the model to the stakes. Most businesses can run 80% on Sonnet, 15% on Haiku, and 5% on Opus.
