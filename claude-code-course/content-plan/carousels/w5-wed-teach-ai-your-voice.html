Step 1

Step 2

Ask: "Describe my writing voice. Tone, phrasing, patterns."

That's the whole prompt. One sentence.

Claude reads everything, identifies your patterns, and creates a detailed voice profile.

Sentence length. Word choices. How you open. How you close. What you never do.

Claude returns a voice profile more accurate than you'd write yourself.

  • Words you overuse (in a good way)
