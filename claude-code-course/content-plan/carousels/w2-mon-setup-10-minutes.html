Step 1

Download the Claude desktop app.

Available for Mac and Windows. Install it like any other app.

Sign in with your Claude account — the same one from claude.ai.

You need a Claude Pro plan ($20/month) or higher.

2 minutes
3 / 8
LOW / CODE
Step 2

Give Claude access to a folder.

In Settings, click "Add folder." Pick a folder with real business documents.

Good choices:

  • Your proposals folder
  • Your client communications
  • Your marketing materials
  • Your SOPs or process docs
1 minute
4 / 8
LOW / CODE
Step 3

Ask Claude to analyze your work.

Try this prompt:

"Read through my recent proposals. What's the typical structure I follow? Summarize my patterns."

Claude reads your actual files and returns your patterns. Not generic advice.

2 minutes
5 / 8
LOW / CODE
Step 4

Make it produce something.

"Based on the structure you found, draft a new proposal for a client named Sarah who needs a Webflow site. Use our standard format and pricing."

Claude uses your template, your pricing, your tone. Not a generic proposal.

3 minutes
6 / 8
LOW / CODE
Step 5 (Optional)

Create your CLAUDE.md file.

Open any text editor. Create a file called CLAUDE.md. Write your business context: who you are, how you talk, your services, your rules.

Save it in your folder. Claude reads it automatically before every conversation.

This is optional for day one — but it's the single biggest upgrade you can make.

15 minutes (but worth it)
