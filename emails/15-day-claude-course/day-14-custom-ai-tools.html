Training Is Step 1. Here's What Step 2 Looks Like.

Everything you've done so far — the knowledge base, the Skills, the Connectors, the prompting — that's deploying Claude as a tool your team uses inside Claude's interface.

That's step 1. It's valuable. It saves time. It improves quality.

Step 2 is different: custom AI tools built specifically for your business.

These are applications that embed AI into your actual workflows. Your team doesn't need to open Claude. The AI is already inside the tools they use every day.

Internal tools (for your team):

  • Custom dashboards — AI-powered analytics that pull from all your systems and surface insights automatically
  • Automated workflows — triggered processes that run without human intervention (new lead comes in, AI qualifies, routes, and drafts initial response)
  • Slack AI assistants — team members ask questions in Slack, AI responds with answers from your knowledge base
  • Process automation — SOPs turned into software that executes the steps, not just documents them

External tools (for your clients and partners):

  • Client portals — self-service dashboards where clients track projects, access documents, and get instant answers
  • AI-powered products — new offerings built on top of your domain expertise
  • Vendor portals — streamlined interfaces for supplier and partner management
  • Custom chatbots — trained on your specific data, not generic responses
The knowledge base you've been building? It's not just for Claude conversations. It becomes the data layer for custom tools. Your SOPs become automated workflows. Your FAQ becomes a client-facing chatbot. Your brand voice guide ensures every automated communication sounds like you.
