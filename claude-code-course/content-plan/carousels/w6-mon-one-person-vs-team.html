Problem 1

5 people, 5 different voices, 5 different pricing quotes.

When everyone uses Claude with their own instructions, you get inconsistent output. One person quotes $15K, another quotes $20K for the same scope.

Proposals sound different. Emails read differently. Your brand fragments.

The fix: shared CLAUDE.md with your pricing, voice, and rules. Everyone reads from the same playbook.

Problem 2

Who should see what?

Your sales team needs pricing and client info. Your content team needs brand guidelines and voice profiles. Your ops team needs SOPs and process docs.

Not everyone needs access to everything. Without structure, either everyone sees too much or nobody can find what they need.
